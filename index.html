userscripts

A small kit for a cleaner, ad-free, more focused web — Facebook & YouTube ad/feed cleaners, a site blocker, and a desktop/mobile view switcher. Tuned against the live DOM, one-click install, and they keep themselves up to date.

⚙️First install a userscript manager — Violentmonkey or Tampermonkey (on Android use Firefox + one of these; on iOS use the Userscripts app for Safari). Then hit Install on any card. Each script carries an @updateURL, so updates arrive automatically — no re-pasting.

🧹 Facebook — Clean Feed

facebook.com

Strips desktop Facebook to just your real newsfeed — hides ads/Sponsored (beats the character-scramble), Stories, Reels trays, Suggested, People-you-may-know and both sidebars; auto-skips Sponsored reels; strips UTM/tracking; forces the chronological feed.

Alt+Shift+F
Installsource

🧹 Facebook Mobile — Clean Feed

m.facebook.com

The mobile-site companion: hides Sponsored, Suggested, People-you-may-know and Reels posts on m.facebook.com, while leaving the mobile navigation intact.

Alt+Shift+F
Installsource

▶️ YouTube — Skip & Hide Ads

youtube.com

Auto-skips pre/mid-roll video ads (clicks Skip, seeks past unskippable ones, mutes), skips Sponsored Shorts, hides feed/banner/overlay ads, and dismisses the “ad blockers not allowed” popup. Works on desktop and mobile web.

Alt+Shift+Y
Installsource

⛔ Site Blocker

any site

Blocks adult sites (always) plus a “Focus Pack” of distractions — social, video, news, shopping — on a work-hours schedule. Add or remove sites and snooze right from the menu.

Alt+Shift+B
Installsource

🖥️ View Mode Switcher

any site

Force any site into Desktop or Mobile rendering — overriding the device signals sites read (user-agent, touch, matchMedia), not just the viewport. On a phone, “Desktop” gives the real desktop layout; on a desktop browser, “Mobile” serves the full-width mobile site. Draggable button (tap to switch, long-press for Auto), keyboard shortcut, or menu. Remembers your choice per site.

Alt+Shift+V
Installsource
